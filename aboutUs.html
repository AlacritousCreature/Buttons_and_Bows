There is nothing better than seeing your loved ones smile wide! A gift might just do that. Whether it be someone's birthday, wedding anniversary, or showering your love by giving them unexpected small gifts in a daily ordinary life.

That's exactly what led to the birth of this venture - Buttons and Bows. We help you gift your loved ones something unique, handmade and something that express your feelings. All of these gifts are tailor made for you and of course handmade in my small little working space. We showcase our art on Instagram and Pinterest. If interested we can be contacted via Instagram DMs and e-mail.

OUR MOTTO
We believe that every day is an occasion to make our loved ones smile. And handmade gifts are unique and personal enough to show your love and effort. Despite that, we know not it's not that easy or quick and we can't always execute it while keeping it a secret.We want to make this process of making handmade gifts easy and fast by providing you with our products that can be filled with your messages, pictures and love later.

WHERE DID THE JOURNEY BEGIN?
Nine months ago, Buttons and Bows began in the small town of Aurangabad, Maharashtra. While the city is known for its historical sites, we want to be known for our unique, handmade and creative products. The founder is a full-time student working on her architecture degree and loves to be creative in her spare time as well.
